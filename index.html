An evening of builders

The 1776 Summit gathers the people building America's hard-power future — private capital, state officials, and defense leaders across space, defense, artificial intelligence, and energy — for one evening of candid conversation in Washington, DC. It is convened by Singh Capital Partners in celebration of the nation's two hundred and fiftieth birthday.
